I made a joke about Chicano food. I get the look I deserve, one pigtail pulled too far. I get kicked in the face in the pit, my mouth tastes like iron. We high five. It's a grey Seattle day, we grab Poke, arguing about the cube rule of food while samples of anime play behind GZA Lyrics.

We live in an era in which we can mix our sense in a million new ways, crossing soundcloud hiphop from Jacksonville with Saam and All Dressed Up chips. We don't live in the world of dean Martin singing about the Moons, Eyes and Pizza Pies while you devour some solid lasagna. We describe all of these experiences in the way: Taste.

This is a space where we're going to talk about trashy pop and 7-11 taquitos, at 2 am, we're going to talk about why punk isn't what it was over pho, (Run The Jewels is punk).

Hang out, lets talk shit.
